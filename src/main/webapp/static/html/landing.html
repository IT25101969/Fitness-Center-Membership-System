Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY
ASKED

Can I try before committing to a plan?
Yes! We offer a free 7-day trial for new members. You'll get full access to our facilities and can attend group classes with no commitment required.
Are there joining fees?
No joining fees, no hidden charges. The monthly plan price is all you pay. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
Can I freeze my membership?
Standard and Premium members can freeze their membership for up to 2 months per year. Perfect for holidays or medical leave.
What equipment is available?
We have 200+ pieces of equipment including Olympic platforms, cable machines, smith machines, 50+ cardio units, and dedicated stretching zones.
Is personal training included?
Personal training is included with Premium membership. Standard and Basic members can add sessions at preferential rates from Rs.1,500 per session.
Do you have parking?
Yes, we have 80 parking spots available for members free of charge, open from 4:30am to 11:30pm daily.
